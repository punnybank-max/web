About the Game

Super Horde blends rogue-like chaos with large-scale strategy. Rise as a newborn monarch, gather heroes, forge alliances, face ancient beasts, and expand your dominion through rolling grasslands, deep forests, frozen ruins, and floating sky-isles.

Game Modes

Campaign: A story-driven progression where your kingdom grows between chapters—recruit heroes, make lasting decisions, and face major scripted encounters that shape later runs.
Skirmish vs AI: Quick, replayable matches against configurable AI opponents. Pick map size, difficulty, and victory conditions for fast tactical play.
Kingdom Deck Builder: Construct a kingdom deck of units, buildings, and policies that defines your strategy each run—discover synergies and refine builds over time.
