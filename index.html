A fast-paced 2D pixel-art rogue-like kingdom builder where you command massive armies and challenge ever-growing AI empires across a high-fantasy world.

About the Game

Super Horde blends rogue-like chaos with large-scale strategy. Rise as a newborn monarch, gather heroes, forge alliances, face ancient beasts, and expand your dominion through rolling grasslands, deep forests, frozen ruins, and floating sky-isles.

Game Modes

Campaign: Follow a branching high-fantasy story, defeat rival kingdoms, and uncover forgotten secrets.
Rogue-like Kingdom Runs: Every attempt reshapes the map with random upgrades, new enemy types, and unpredictable challenges.
AI Conquest: Massive auto-battles against evolving AI empires that rebuild, counter your army, and attack your borders.
